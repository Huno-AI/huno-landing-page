Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Huno AI ("Huno," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Huno mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Eligibility

The App is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a person under 18, we will delete that information immediately. If you are under 18, do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and, if you use social login (Sign in with Apple or Google), basic profile information provided by those services (name and email). Conversation Data: When you interact with Huno through Talk, Chat, or Journal modes, your conversation content is processed to generate responses. Conversations are associated with your account to maintain context within sessions.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized, aggregated usage data such as which modes you use and session duration. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Payment Information: Subscription payments are processed entirely by Apple through In-App Purchases. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card number, bank account details, or other financial information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features
  • Process your conversations and generate AI responses
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Communicate with you about service updates or respond to support requests
  • Monitor for and prevent abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms of Service

4. What We Do NOT Do

We want to be explicitly clear about the following:

  • We do not sell your personal information. Not now, not ever.
  • We do not display advertisements. The App is entirely ad-free.
  • We do not build advertising profiles. Your data is never used for targeted advertising or shared with advertisers.
  • We do not share your conversation content with third parties except as required to operate the service (see Section 5).
  • We do not use your conversations to train AI models that are made available to other users or third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase: Authentication and account management. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Cloud infrastructure for hosting and AI model inference. Subject to AWS's Privacy Policy.
  • ElevenLabs: Voice synthesis for Talk mode. Voice interaction data is sent to ElevenLabs for text-to-speech processing. Subject to ElevenLabs' Privacy Policy.
  • Apple: In-App Purchase processing and App Store distribution. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We require all third-party service providers to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data and to use it only for the purposes of providing their services to us.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and conversation data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated personal information and conversation data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you may be retained indefinitely for service improvement purposes.

7. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest, secure authentication, and access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
